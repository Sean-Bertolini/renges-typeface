Old Blockface

My font is known as Old Blockface. The reason behind the name is that there is a lot of inspiration behind the font drawn from an Old English style of text, however the text is done in an 8 bit block style. The typeface still has the looks of the elegant and detailed serifs that the Old English font had, however it is finished not in the smoth curves of Old English, but in the blocky squares and rectangles of what a classic 8-Bit video game would look like.
